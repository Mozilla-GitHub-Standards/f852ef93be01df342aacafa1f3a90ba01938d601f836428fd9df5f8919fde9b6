How has working with ______ been? What have they done well? What do you think could be improved? If you'd like, you can structure your feedback in a few areas:

* Skill Level/Technical Ability - how difficult are the kinds of problems they solve?
* Productivity/Output - how much shippable, valuable, finished work do they get done?
* Group Contribution - how much do they contribute to amplifying colleagues, improving workflow, or writing tools used by others?
* Product Contribution - how much does their work matter to ___? Do they prioritize time and resources well? Do they understand our users?
